Crowdfunding your dream house

with the help of your friends, families and trusted people

ABOUT CFHP is a free service which allows you to collect and manage micro-funds to build or buy your own house. Instead of taking traditional loans from banks which require certain credit scores and have different interest rates, you can finance your project from multiple individuals who are willing to invest smaller amount of capital for an agreed return profit.

Estimate Costs Use the Calculator below to estimate the total cost of a project and determine your repayment timeline.
Find Investors Learn about who are potential investors and how to find and involve them in your housing project.
Agree on Terms Use our services to create a project plan, set goals and create agreements between you and the investors.
Maintain Project Use the CFHP Admin Tools to keep track of the progress of your ongoing projects and to create reports.
